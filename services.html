Our Services

We provide comprehensive eye care for patients of all ages using modern diagnostic technology and personalized treatment plans.

Adult Eye Care

The American Optometric Association recommends eye exams every one to two years depending on age and health.

Our exams include evaluation of:

  • Eye alignment
  • Pupil testing and reflexes
  • Visual field screening
  • Vision testing (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, presbyopia)
  • Screening for cataracts, glaucoma, and macular degeneration

Pediatric Eye Care

Early detection of vision problems supports learning and development. School screenings do not replace a comprehensive eye exam.

Signs your child may benefit from an eye exam:

  • Squinting
  • Frequent headaches
  • Eyes pointing in different directions
  • Blurred or double vision
  • Tilting the head to favor one eye
  • Holding books too close or too far away
  • Frequent blinking or eye rubbing
  • Difficulty judging distances
  • Poor school or athletic performance

We accept children beginning at age 6.

Advanced Diagnostic Technology

  • Retinal imaging and documentation allow detailed monitoring of eye health and support early detection of many eye conditions.
  • Our modern diagnostic equipment helps provide accurate evaluations while maintaining high standards of patient care.
